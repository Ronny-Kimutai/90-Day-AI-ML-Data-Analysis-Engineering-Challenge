Overview:
This week focused on strengthening core Python skills and building a solid foundation in data handling using Pandas and NumPy. The goal was to become fully comfortable loading, inspecting, cleaning, transforming, and preparing engineering datasets for later analysis and machine learning.

Key Focus Areas:

1. Dataset Loading & Exploration
- Imported multiple engineering datasets (CSV).
- Reviewed dataset structure using head(), info(), describe().
- Checked for missing values, incorrect datatypes, outliers, and duplicates.

2. Data Cleaning
- Removed duplicates across datasets.
- Handled missing values via dropping or filling.
- Corrected datatype issues, especially timestamps and numerical columns.

3. Timestamp & Time-Series Preparation

- Extracted time-based features: hour of day, day of week, month.
- Calculated engineering metrics such as charging duration, time intervals, power/energy indicators.

- Charging duration (hours)

- Daily energy totals
- Sensor deltas between readings

- Vehicle types
- Charger types
- Days of the week
- Hours of the day
- Sensor categories
- Locations/stations
